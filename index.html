What the family practices

  • Spotting cropped evidence and missing context
  • Separating "possible" from "proven"
  • Asking for the original source before sharing
  • Using a calm script when a rumor feels urgent

Required artifact

Family Escape Plan

Every buyer gets a printable card and phone-note version for online threats, manipulation, humiliation, or panic.

For kids

  1. Stop replying.
  2. Do not send more.
  3. Do not pay.
  4. Save evidence.
  5. Tell one safe adult immediately.

For parents

  1. Say: "You are not in trouble right now."
  2. Get calm before getting curious.
  3. Preserve messages and screenshots.
  4. Do not lead with punishment.
  5. Stay physically with a panicking kid.
